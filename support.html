When reporting a problem, it helps to include your device model, your phone's operating system version, and a short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.

Common questions

My Impulse Edge won't connect

  • Make sure Bluetooth is turned on and the app has Bluetooth permission in your device settings.
  • Check the device is charged and powered on.
  • Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your phone's Bluetooth.

Account & data

  • You can delete your account and its associated data at any time from Settings → Delete account in the app.
  • For details on what we collect and how it's used, see our Privacy Policy.
